## How to Use This

1. **Copy** this template as `weekly-review-[DATE].md`
2. **Fill in** all fields honestly
3. **Time it** - should take 10-15 minutes
4. **Save** in `docs/learnings/`
5. **Commit** with message like `[weekly] Review week of 2026-03-09`
6. **Update** `OPTIMIZATIONS.md` if you discovered new improvement ideas

## Tips

- Be specific (not "hard" but "hard to organize sparks by topic")
- Celebrate wins (what went well is just as important)
- Capture ideas quickly (don't over-analyze)
- Commit the review (creates audit trail of progress)
