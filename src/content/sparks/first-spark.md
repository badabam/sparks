This is your first spark - a moment of insight to capture and share.

Sparks are brief, focused learning moments. Think of them as:
- Quick discoveries
- Aha moments
- Interesting patterns you've noticed
- Small lessons learned

Keep them short. Keep them genuine. Let them spark conversation.

Ready to write your next spark? Add a new markdown file in `src/content/sparks/` to get started.
